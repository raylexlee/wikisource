Memoirs of a Woman of Pleasure  (1749) 
by John Cleland
  • sister projects: Wikipedia article, Commons category, Wikidata item.

Memoirs of a Woman of Pleasure, also known as Fanny Hill is a novel by John Cleland. Written in 1749 while Cleland was in debtor's prison in London, it is considered the first erotic novel, and has become a byword for the battle of censorship of erotica.— Excerpted from Fanny Hill on Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia.

Each volume consists of one letter written by Fanny Hill. For convenience each has been subdivided into five smaller parts.
